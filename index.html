Your Waste, Our Responsibility.


An app for managing all types of waste


Klear is a platform that enables people to sell things which have expired or have been left untouched or are of no use to them. Consumers can avail our services through the means of the Klear™ app. Users can use our Machine Learning based image classifier to classify the type of waste into rubber,plastic,glass,cardboard,e-waste etc and then according to the output of the classifier, they can schedule a pickup according to their comfort.A representative picks up the items at the requested time. It takes 2-3 business days for the evaluation process to take place. The evaluated price is then credited to the user's account. The user can then avail gift cards, vouchers, or transfer the credits to their bank account. They also have an option to donate the amount credited to their account to charity. We also have the feature of DIYs which gives unique and creative ideas of best out of waste.


Klear has the potential to reduce unjudicious disposing of waste and lower the amount of trash goes non-recycled.
